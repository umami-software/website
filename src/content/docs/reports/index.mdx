# Reports

Umami has built-in reporting to help you gain deeper insights into your data.
Together, these reports offer a comprehensive understanding of user behavior, satisfaction, and opportunities for optimization.

## Available reports

- [Insights](/docs/reports/report-insights) - Dive deeper into your data by using segments and filters.
- [Funnel](/docs/reports/report-funnel) - Understand the conversion and drop-off rate of users.
- [Retention](/docs/reports/report-retention) - Measure your website stickiness by tracking how often users return.
- [UTM](/docs/reports/report-utm) - Track your campaigns through UTM parameters.
- [Goals](/docs/reports/report-goals) - Track your goals for pageviews and events.
- [Journey](/docs/reports/report-journey) - Understand how users nagivate through your website.

## Create a report

Reports are accessible after clicking **View** on a website.

Navigate to **Reports** and click on the **Create report** button.

<img src="https://content.umami.is/website/images/docs/v2/report-create.png" />

## Accessing a report

You can access all reports or website specific reports by clicking on their respective **Reports** button.

Navigate to **Reports** and click on the **View** button.
